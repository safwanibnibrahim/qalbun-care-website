Concerns We Support

Individual counselling may support concerns such as grief and loss, anxiety, depression, addiction or unwanted habits, anger, stress, pre-marriage and post-marriage concerns, self-esteem, relationships, studies, career, personal growth, decision-making, faith, identity, procrastination, and more.

Consultations may cover mental health education, parenting, child development, student guidance, classroom and behaviour strategies, caregiver training, and related concerns.

Payment & Financial Aid

All payments are to be made before the session. Payments are non-refundable for cancellations made with less than 24 hours' notice before the scheduled session.

Financial aid may be available for those in need. To apply, email qalbun.care@outlook.com.
