Why Qalbun Care?

Our Inspiration

What Inspired Qalbun Care

The name Qalbun Care was inspired by this prophetic teaching, which reminds us of the central role the heart plays in our lives. When the heart is troubled, it can affect how we think, feel, relate to others, and navigate life's challenges. Through professional counselling and compassionate support, we hope to help individuals find healing, growth, and inner peace.
